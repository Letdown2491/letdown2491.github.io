About The Network

The Nerdy Legion Network is a place for people obsessed with geek culture with topics ranging from comics, TV, movies, wrestling and more. All our hosts aim to bring you high quality, relevant conversation relating to geek culture in a unique and powerful voice without ever sugar coating the topic at hand.

The Nerdy Legion Network was co-founded in July 2015 and since its inception has become a destination for people looking for great content and our network has grown through engaging the community and bringing on podcast listeners as hosts for new shows. Our founders have over 20 years of combined experience in the podcasting industry and lifelong dedication to the topics discussed.

Since 2015, the network has grown from just three shows to over twenty different podcasts covering all aspects of geek and nerd culture. Whether it's comics, books, TV, film, wrestling, or the odd hobby, the Nerdy Legion Network has a show you will enjoy, created by passionate people who love what they do.

You can get in touch by sending us an email or getting in touch via Twitter. You can find individual contact information for all our hosts via the Subscribe page as well. We hope you enjoy listening to our content as much as we love creating it.
